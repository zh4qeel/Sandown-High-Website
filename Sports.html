School Sports Activities

Sports We Offer

Soccer

Our soccer team competes in local leagues and tournaments. Training sessions are held twice a week with experienced coaches.

Practice Schedule: Mondays & Wednesdays, 4:00 - 6:00 PM

Chess

The chess club offers a quiet, focused environment for students to practice and develop their strategic thinking skills.

Practice Schedule: Thursdays, 3:30 - 5:00 PM

Netball

Our netball team is active in inter-school competitions. We encourage players of all skill levels to join and participate.

Practice Schedule: Tuesdays & Thursdays, 4:00 - 5:30 PM

Basketball

Basketball players enjoy rigorous training sessions and competitive games. Beginners and experienced players are welcome!

Practice Schedule: Mondays & Fridays, 4:00 - 6:00 PM

Athletics

Our athletics program includes track and field events. Students can train and compete in various disciplines, from sprints to long-distance races.

Practice Schedule: Tuesdays & Thursdays, 4:00 - 6:00 PM
